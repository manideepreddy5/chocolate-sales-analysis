# Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze chocolate sales performance across products, regions, and time. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, compare performance trends, and identify high-performing products and sales contributors.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze chocolate sales data to identify key revenue drivers, evaluate sales performance across regions and products, and support data-driven business decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** $140M
* **Total Boxes Sold:** 9M
* **Total Profit:** $80M
* **Profit Percentage:** 57.4%

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

## Features & Analysis

### 1. Year-over-Year Performance (CY vs PY)

* Compared **Current Year vs Previous Year** for:

  * Revenue (Amount)
  * Sales Volume (Boxes)
* Identified trends, growth patterns, and seasonal fluctuations.

### 2. Shipment Distribution

* Visualized distribution of shipment quantities.
* Helps understand:

  * Order frequency
  * Volume spread
  * Operational load patterns

### 3. Top Products Analysis

* Identified **Top 6 revenue-generating products**:

  * Organic Choco Syrup
  * Caramel Stuffed Bars
  * Smooth Silky Chocolates
  * 99% Dark Chocolate
  * Almond Chocolates
  * 70% Dark Bites
* Highlights key revenue drivers.

### 4. Salesperson Performance

* Ranked **Top 6 salespersons** based on:

  * Total revenue
  * Total boxes sold
  * Profit percentage
* Helps evaluate individual performance and contribution.

### 5. Product Performance Table

* Detailed view of:

  * Product-wise revenue
  * Profit percentage
* Enables comparison of profitability across products.

### 6. Geographic Analysis

* Revenue distribution across regions:

  * India
  * New Zealand
  * Australia
  * USA
  * UK
  * Canada
* Identifies high-performing markets and regional opportunities.

### 7. Time-Based Filtering

* Interactive date slicer:

  * Allows dynamic analysis across time periods
  * Supports trend-based decision-making

## Key Insights

* A small set of products contributes significantly to total revenue.
* Profit margins vary across different product categories.
* Certain salespersons consistently outperform others.
* Geographic regions show varied sales performance.
* Sales trends indicate seasonal fluctuations in demand.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products to maximize revenue.
* Optimize pricing strategies for low-margin products.
* Provide incentives to high-performing salespersons to boost productivity.
* Expand operations in high-performing regions.
* Improve supply chain planning based on shipment distribution patterns.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions. It helps in identifying growth opportunities, improving operational efficiency, and enhancing overall business strategy.
